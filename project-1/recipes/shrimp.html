Garlic Butter Sauteed Shrimp

This pan-fried shrimp is tossed with an easy Italian seasoning and then saut√©ed in butter and lemon. Though we often enjoy breaded shrimp such as our bang bang shrimp recipe, this pan sauteed shrimp is completely ready in 10 minutes, perfect for quick dinners!

Sauteed Shrimp Seasoning

  • Italian seasoning: a blend of herbs used to flavor many Italian dishes.
  • Salt and pepper: classic seasoning for any dish.
  • Paprika: It adds a hint of smoky mild sweetness.
  • One whole lemon: to squeeze onto the shrimp after cooking.

How to Saute Shrimp

  1. Devein and remove the tail of shrimp (I kept my tail on b/c they look better in pictures).
  2. In a bowl, combine the ingredients for the seasonings. Whisk together the Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Then in a skillet, add the butter and garlic, saute until the garlic is fragrant (30 seconds to 1 minute), stirring frequently.
  4. Add seasoned shrimp and cook 2-3 minutes per side over medium heat.
  5. Remove from heat and drizzle freshly squeezed lemon over the entire dish.
  6. Enjoy!
